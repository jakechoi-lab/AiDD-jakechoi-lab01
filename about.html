My Journey

Hello! I'm Jake Choi, a passionate entrepreneur and student currently pursuing my Master's in Information Systems (MSIS) at Indiana University. My background spans entrepreneurship and corporate innovation, where I've developed a keen eye for identifying opportunities and creating solutions that bridge technology and business.

My academic pursuit in MSIS complements my entrepreneurial endeavors, providing me with the technical foundation and business acumen needed to drive meaningful change in today's digital landscape.

I believe in the power of continuous learning, strategic innovation, and the importance of building solutions that truly make a difference. Whether I'm working on Milae Golf, diving into my coursework, or exploring new technologies, I approach every challenge with curiosity and determination.
